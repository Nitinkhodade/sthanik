About Us

Sthanik Technology Solutions LLP is an online marketplace & our product www.sthanik.com and mobile applications are cloud platform solution available for end consumers, local retailers and distributors at no cost, targeting both B2C and B2B e-commerce marketplace. We aim to simplify the end to end supply chain cycle.

Our goal is to bring end consumer, local retailer, wholesale distributor, manufacturer and many more interested parties on to a single common platform to sell or buy goods online.

Sthanik Technology Solutions does not produce, distribute, sell or deliver any products; we help retailers host their products online on Sthanik platform and allow end consumers to purchase them online.

Consumer Products

You as an end consumer always buy your needs from your nearby stores. With us you continue to purchase those same items from that same store Online. We connect you to all shops which serve grocery, food, fruits, vegetables, bakery Items, cosmetic and much more. This saves you from going through ordinary daily need shopping experience and transition to a seamless online access of your favorite store at the tip of your fingers. Allow Sthanik to work on managing your daily needs and we assure your time is spent on things that matters most to you.

Vendor Products

Sthanik makes it easier for retailers, distributors, manufacturers to host your products online. We help you streamline the process of managing inventory, showcasing products online, managing orders, delivery, invoicing and payouts. All these services together on a single platform increases your efficiency, saves operation cost and provides better control on your business.
